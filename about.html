FAISAL AL-NAGHMOSH | ABOUT


Faisal Al-Naghmosh Trading and Industry is a fast-growing Saudi Arabian conglomerate. The company has played a key role in the development of Saudi Arabia's infrastructure for more than four decades. It continues to contribute to the Saudi Arabian nation's progress and prosperity today.


Our Real Estate Sector makes large-scale investments in land and property development, and our Investment Sector forms various commercial agreements with worldwide firms.


Trading, Industries, Smart Fleet Technology, Meat, Manufacturing, Bakery Products, Milk Products, and Food Products are the eight Business Areas in which the group operates.


The company takes pleasure in not only providing products and services of the greatest quality and standards, but also in maintaining the highest level of integrity with everyone with whom it does business.
